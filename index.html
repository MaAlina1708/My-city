Everything starts in Kyiv



Kyiv is one of the largest and oldest cities in Europe. Its one and a half thousand year history is shrouded in myths and legends. Kyiv is based on the slopes of the Dnieper, one of the most powerful waterways in Eastern Europe. Today, more than 3 million people live in the capital of Ukraine.
